What is The Field?

Started as a passion project to employ the use of machine learning and predictive modeling, The Field provides forecasts across popular sports competitions:

  • La Liga
  • NBA
  • MLS
  • Champions League
  • Barclays Premier League

Models are built using historical team and player-level stats to understand drivers of wins and losses in major soccer and basketball leagues and tournaments. A supplementary model is in progress to transform sports news data into probabilistic inputs for model fine-tuning. The goal is to quantify events such as injuries, transfers, and tactical lineup changes to add realism to model outputs. I will continue to fine-tune inputs, and make improvements to better understand how to accurately model sports data.
